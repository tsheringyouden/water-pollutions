🧪 Common Causes Industrial Discharges: Factories release toxic chemicals and heavy metals into water bodies. Agricultural Runoff: Fertilizers, pesticides, and animal waste wash into streams and rivers. Urbanization: Stormwater carries trash, oil, and chemicals from cities into waterways. Improper Waste Disposal: Dumping plastics, pharmaceuticals, and household waste pollutes water. Oil Spills: Accidental leaks from ships or drilling operations severely damage marine ecosystems3. 🐠 Effects on the Environment Aquatic Life: Pollutants can kill fish, disrupt food chains, and destroy habitats. Eutrophication: Excess nutrients cause algal blooms that deplete oxygen and suffocate marine life. Human Health: Contaminated water can spread diseases and toxins, affecting millions globally. 🌱 How to Prevent It Reduce plastic use and recycle properly Support sustainable farming and waste management Conserve water and avoid dumping chemicals down drains Advocate for stronger pollution control policies
